# **App Name**: Quantix Growth Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Clear CTA: A compelling hero section with a clear headline, subheadline, and a prominent WhatsApp CTA button.
- Positioning Statement: A section highlighting Quantix as a strategic growth partner with a focus on data-driven strategies.
- Service Cards with Benefit-Focused Descriptions: Cards detailing services like traffic management, website creation, and sales funnel automation, emphasizing client benefits.
- Differentiators Section: A section outlining Quantix's unique selling points, such as customized strategies and a focus on ROI.
- Authority Proof: A section providing authority proof through impactful phrases and placeholder testimonials or client logos.
- Final Persuasive CTA: A strong final call to action reinforcing urgency and opportunity with a direct WhatsApp integration.
- Lead Qualification: System to score incoming leads and rank their compatibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark navy blue (#192A56) to convey a sense of premium quality and reliability.
- Background color: Very dark gray (#222222) to create a modern and high-tech feel, making the lighter elements stand out.
- Accent color: Neon cyan (#00FFFF) to highlight key interactive elements like buttons and links, conveying innovation and technology.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a modern, clean, and corporate look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) providing excellent readability and complementing the professional tone.
- Modern line icons representing each service in a clean and professional manner.
- Subtle animations such as scroll reveal, fade-in effects, and microinteractions to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
- A clean, minimal layout that ensures scannability and focuses user attention on the conversion goals.
